### Summary

David Orban and Max Pog discussed the development of a new matchmaking platform aimed at simplifying the fundraising process for early-stage startups and investors. Max emphasized the market opportunity for a platform that connects these two parties directly, allowing for seamless booking of calls without prior approval. They highlighted the importance of onboarding investors with deployable capital and ensuring their understanding of the platform. Additionally, David stressed the need for effective execution and preparation for investor meetings, while also discussing the significance of having a data room ready to maintain fundraising momentum. They concluded with plans for Max to connect with Ray Stalks regarding data room preparation and the potential use of webinars to enhance investor engagement.
