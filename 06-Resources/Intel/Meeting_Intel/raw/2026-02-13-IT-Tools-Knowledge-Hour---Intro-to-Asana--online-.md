### Summary

The workshop introduced Asana’s core concepts (tasks, projects, portfolios), views (list, board, timeline/Gantt), and collaboration features including comments, @mentions, rules/automation, and reporting. The presenter demonstrated practical project examples: a ticketing workflow, a detailed onboarding template, and a website development project, highlighting templates and reproducible improvements. The session showcased AI integrations for extracting tasks from transcripts and auto-generating projects, and addressed participant questions about structuring projects, permissions, subtasks, and daily workflows. Actionable follow-ups include an offline, case-specific walkthrough for a participant and continued adoption practices shared by experienced users.
