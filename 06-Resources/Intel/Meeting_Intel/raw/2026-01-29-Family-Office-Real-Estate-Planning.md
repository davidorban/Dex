### Summary

The meeting covered intake and assessment of a large incoming family-office real-estate portfolio, emphasizing the need to capture property histories, operators, rents, and legal encumbrances. Participants agreed on a staged approach: receive physical boxes of documents (mostly Arabic), prioritize a small set of high-priority properties for depth-first manual assessment to learn quirks, then scale with targeted automation. They discussed secure document handling (Trezorit invites and folder access), future tooling (DocuSign, Expensify), and operational security measures including deploying OnePassword and a short security briefing for device and data practices.
