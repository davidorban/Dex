### Summary

The meeting introduced the 'quiet capital' concept (social, network, domain, purpose, community) and demonstrated an enterprise tool that automates LinkedIn scraping, lead-list building, outbound social campaigns, and conversion tracking. The presenter showed how to identify ICPs and competitors, scrape post interactions, and run auto-comment campaigns with conversion classification. Operational advice was given: start small (50/day), monitor initial lists (recommend first 3,000), and iterate based on data. Attendees provided feedback about retesting the tool, AI voice fidelity, and scheduling follow-ups; the presenter arranged next-step logistics via Francesca's Calendly.
