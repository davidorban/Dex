### Summary

The meeting covered IT strategy, compliance, AI usage, scalable data platforms, and fostering an IT-aware culture. Key operational decisions included designating SharePoint for policy documents and Asana for auditable task records; budgeting for select AI tools while restricting AI in regulated advice unless anonymized via a dedicated ADGM-hosted anonymization system; and building Microsoft-based dashboards integrating accounting data. Practical usability changes were prioritized to reduce cognitive load (removing external email footers and mobile signatures) and applying sensible sensitivity labels. The team will publish an intranet, circulate security and acceptable-use documents for employee sign-off, standardize Excel templates for later Power BI integration, and emphasize departmental ownership for cultivating IT culture.
