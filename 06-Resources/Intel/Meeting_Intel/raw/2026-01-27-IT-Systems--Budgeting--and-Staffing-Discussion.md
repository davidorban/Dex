### Summary

The meeting focused on structuring IT, budgets, and staffing to support wealth management and family office services. David presented three budget inputs (accounting, division tool requests, and IT realities), highlighted large potential enterprise costs (BlackRock/Aladdin/EFront, Microsoft Dynamics) and questioned incumbent vendor bias. Immediate operational fixes (password manager, expense approvals, SharePoint intranet) and hiring plans (onboarding Shores, transitional roles, headcount timing) were discussed. Participants distinguished reporting capabilities from execution/trading requirements, emphasized bespoke needs for family office property/business management, and agreed to refine the living budget and procurement approach collaboratively while prioritizing earlier needs for Alcaf client services over later fund execution systems.
